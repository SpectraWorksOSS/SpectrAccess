# MODIS/VIIRS Calibration LUT Data Terms

The spectrAccess MODIS/VIIRS calibration connector is a fetch mechanism for
source calibration lookup products and does not redistribute source data.

Users are responsible for complying with NOAA STAR VIIRS product terms and any
NASA/MCST MODIS access terms that apply to files they fetch with their own
access. The MODIS side is intentionally a documented stub until a web-only
access design is implemented.
